Challenges in India's Healthcare System

Lack of Infrastructure

Many hospitals in rural areas lack basic facilities like beds, medical equipment, or even clean water.

Delayed Ambulances

Ambulances often fail to reach patients on time, especially in remote locations, leading to preventable deaths.

Shortage of Doctors

A significant shortage of trained medical professionals leads to overburdened staff and inadequate care for patients.

High Treatment Costs

The high cost of healthcare makes it inaccessible for many people, especially those living below the poverty line.
